2.4.1 Boolean logic questions

What this topic covers: reading logic diagrams, writing Boolean expressions, drawing circuits and completing truth tables using AND, OR and NOT gates.

1) Write the Boolean expression represented by Figure 1. (3 marks)

Mark scheme

  • Identifies A AND B.
  • Identifies the result is ORed with C.
  • Correct expression: Q = (A AND B) OR C.

5) Draw a logic gate diagram for Q = (A OR B) AND C. (4 marks)

Mark scheme

Award up to 4 marks:

  • Correctly labels inputs A, B and C.
  • Uses an OR gate with inputs A and B.
  • Uses an AND gate to combine the OR output with C.
  • Correctly labels the final output Q and wires the gates in the correct order.

6) Draw a logic gate diagram for Q = (A AND NOT B) OR NOT C. (5 marks)

Mark scheme

Award up to 5 marks:

  • Correctly labels inputs A, B and C.
  • Uses a NOT gate on input B.
  • Uses an AND gate to combine A and NOT B.
  • Uses a NOT gate on input C.
  • Uses an OR gate to combine the AND output with NOT C and labels the output Q.

7) Figure 4 shows a logic circuit used in an alarm system. The alarm output Q is 1 only for certain input combinations. Explain how the circuit produces Q. (4 marks)

Mark scheme

1 mark for each correct point:

  • B is passed through a NOT gate to produce NOT B.
  • A and NOT B are combined using an OR gate.
  • C is passed through a NOT gate to produce NOT C.
  • The OR output and NOT C are combined using an AND gate, so Q = (A OR NOT B) AND NOT C.
  • Accept any other valid point.

8) Identify which diagram, Figure 5 or Figure 6, represents Q = NOT((A AND B) OR C). (2 marks)

Mark scheme

Award up to 2 marks:

  • Figure 5 is correct.
  • Figure 5 applies NOT to the whole result of (A AND B) OR C.
  • Figure 6 represents Q = NOT(A AND B) OR C.

9) A student writes Q = NOT A AND B OR C for Figure 7. Identify the error and write the corrected expression. (3 marks)

Mark scheme

Award up to 3 marks:

  • The student has not shown that B OR C is grouped together before the final AND gate.
  • Correct expression: Q = NOT A AND (B OR C).
  • Equivalent expression accepted: Q = (NOT A) AND (B OR C).
  • Accept any other valid point.

10) Describe the role of AND, OR and NOT gates in Boolean logic. (3 marks)

Mark scheme

1 mark for each correct description:

  • AND outputs 1 only when all its inputs are 1.
  • OR outputs 1 when at least one input is 1.
  • NOT reverses/inverts the input, so 1 becomes 0 and 0 becomes 1.
